DigiBillMate eliminates these problems by converting every printed bill into a secure digital copy ‚Äî automatically, silently, and without changing your existing billing software or workflow.

Who Is DigiBillMate For?

Retail stores, supermarkets, pharmacies, restaurants, service centers, clinics, and any business that prints customer bills at a physical counter.

How DigiBillMate Works

DigiBillMate quietly watches your billing system and detects each bill the moment it is generated.

The bill is instantly converted into a secure digital copy with watermarking applied as per your configuration.

A cloud-hosted version is created with a short access link, ensuring fast and reliable delivery.

The customer automatically receives the digital bill via WhatsApp or SMS ‚Äî without any manual action.

All operations run silently in the background, without slowing down the billing counter.
