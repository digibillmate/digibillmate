What DigiBillMate Solves

How DigiBillMate Works

  1. Bill is generated in your existing billing software
  2. DigiBillMate automatically detects the bill PDF
  3. A secure digital copy is created with watermark
  4. Bill is uploaded to cloud storage with short link
  5. Customer receives the bill instantly via WhatsApp or SMS
