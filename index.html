What DigiBillMate Solves

Even today, most billing counters rely entirely on printed bills. This results in paper waste, lost invoices, customer disputes, and zero post-billing engagement.

DigiBillMate eliminates these problems by converting every printed bill into a secure digital copy — automatically, silently, and without changing your existing billing software or workflow.

No retraining. No replacement. No operational disruption.

Who Is DigiBillMate For?

Retail stores, supermarkets, pharmacies, restaurants, service centers, clinics, and any business that prints customer bills at a physical counter.

Especially suited for high-volume environments where speed, reliability, and zero disruption matter.

How DigiBillMate Works (Billing Automation)

DigiBillMate detects each bill the moment it is generated.

It converts the bill into a secure digital copy.

Creates a fast-access cloud link.

Automatically delivers it via WhatsApp or SMS.

Runs silently in the background without slowing billing operations.
